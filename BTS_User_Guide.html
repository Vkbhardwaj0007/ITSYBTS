1. Getting Started

Hardware Installation

Ensure your itsybts Home Control board is properly wired by a professional. The board supports:

  • 4-Channel Lighting Control (On/Off)
  • 2-Channel Fan Control (Speed Dimming)
  • 2-Way Physical Switch Support

Wiring Diagram

* Note: Common wire must be connected to Neutral for safe operation.

2. App Connectivity

Step 1: Download & Login

Install the ITSYBTS TECHNOLOGY App. Log in using your credentials (BTSTECH ID). Master Admins can bypass serial checks.

Step 2: Connecting to Device

The system uses Dual-Mode Connectivity (Bluetooth + WiFi):

  • Bluetooth: Turn on Phone Bluetooth. Select your device from the scan list (e.g., "BTS_HOME_NODE_01").
  • WiFi Setup: Once connected via Bluetooth, enter your home WiFi SSID and Password in the App settings. The device will now connect to the itsybts Cloud.

3. How to Control

Dashboard Controls

  • Lights: Toggle buttons on the dashboard to turn lights ON/OFF instantly.
  • Fans: Use the Slider or the (+) / (-) icons to adjust fan speed smoothly without any humming noise.
  • Room Scenes: Group devices into different rooms (Main Office, Kitchen, Bedroom) for easier management.

App-Based Scheduling

Set "Schedules" directly from the side menu to automate your lights. For example, you can set your outdoor lights to automatically turn ON at 6:30 PM every day through the app.

4. Safety Features

  • Real-time Sync: If you use a physical switch, the App status updates automatically.
  • Overload Protection: Inbuilt fuse and thermal protection via high-grade heatsinks.
  • Offline Mode: Even if your Internet is down, you can still control devices via local Bluetooth.
