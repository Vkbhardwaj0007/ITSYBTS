Engineering Department

The Innovation Core of ITSYBTS TECHNOLOGY

Advanced Embedded Systems & R&D

At ITSYBTS TECHNOLOGY, the Engineering Department is the center of our innovation. We don't just build products; we design the future of smart connectivity and industrial intelligence.

Our work spans from the low-level architectural design of microcontrollers to the high-level integration of cloud-based industrial ecosystems. We bridge the gap between physical hardware and digital intelligence.

Hardware Design

Complex PCB multi-layer routing, RF design, and power management circuits for IoT and Industrial use.

Firmware Engineering

Optimized C/C++ development for STM32, ESP32, and RTOS-based real-time control systems.

What We Do at itsybts Engineering

01. PROTOTYPING & R&D

We convert ideas into working hardware. Using advanced simulation and rapid prototyping techniques, we test concepts for feasibility and performance before full-scale design.

02. INDUSTRIAL AUTOMATION

Designing smart control panels, custom PLCs, and sensor networks that communicate via Modbus, RS485, and MQTT for industrial digital transformation.

03. SMART HOME ECOSYSTEMS

Engineering the core circuits for touch switches, automated lighting systems, and experimental kinetic displays that define modern living spaces.
